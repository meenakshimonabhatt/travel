about us

ABOUT BIAS ADVENTURERS Exploring amazing travel destinations across the BHIMTAL, new culture, lifestyle, traditions and travelling to some of the exciting destinations on this earth is something that can make the busy and boring life of the people interesting.BIAS ADVENTURERS is all about unusual and exciting activities that offer ultimate fun and experience.

Whether adventure means a kayaking , boating, paragliding or trekking and camping on the high mountains, we assure a safe yet thrilling adventure experience to our travellers. Meticulous planning, safety and eco-sense mix together help us to add on smiles on the face of our every traveller. Our prime objective is to offer cost effective adventure trips to all the passionate and daring travellers located across the BHIMTAL.

At BIAS ADVENTURERS, we are pleased to have an experienced and adventurous team that is always ready to assist our guests and help them in experiencing the real fun and adventure. We are well-acknowledged for offering an extensive range of holiday destinations and adventure activities. Also, we are capable of customizing the travelling experience of our clients according to their budget and needs.
